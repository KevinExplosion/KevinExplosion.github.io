Calorie & Meal Tracker

A meal tracker built using Angular2. The app enables you to add meals to a list of meals with a title, description, and calorie amount. You can edit items on the list, or delete items on the list.

Relevant Technologies:

  • Angular2
  • SASS
  • HTML
